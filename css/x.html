Gum Surgery in NYC and New Jersey

Laser Smile Correction

Laser gum reshaping procedures are essentially painless and bloodless, while requiring little or no anesthesia. Laser procedures are used for crown lengthening and to correct a gummy smile. See what Laser gum surgery can do for you!

The Pinhole Procedure

The Pinhole Procedure is the best minimally-invasive gum surgery alternative, as it does not require incisions or stitches and virutally no recovery time!

Laser Gum Therapy

A minimally invasive alternative to traditional gum surgery, laser gum treatment is an easier, faster-healing treatment for gum disease. Many patients are choosing laser gum therapy (or LANAP™), as no incisions or stitches are required - LANAP™ elminates fear!
